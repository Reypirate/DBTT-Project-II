# About Our Web App

This application is a specialized heritage platform for **Hin Long Joss Sticks & Papers**, designed to bridge traditional Chinese rituals with modern technology to help families honor their ancestors with cultural accuracy.

## Customer Side

### Overall Features

- **Heritage Calendar**: Automated tracking of both **Gregorian and Lunar dates** for major festivals such as **Qingming (Tomb Sweeping)**, **Hungry Ghost Month**, and deity-specific days like **Guan Yin's Enlightenment Day**.
- **Specialized Product Catalog**:
  - **Curated Bundles**: Pre-packaged kits like the **Qingming Essential Kit ($38.00)**, **7th Month Hungry Ghost Bundle ($45.00)**, and **CNY Wealth & Prosperity Set ($55.00)**.
  - **Standalone Offerings**: Individual items such as Premium Gold Joss Paper, Sandalwood Incense, Giant Red Candles, and Paper Clothing.
- **AI Heritage Advisor**: A TanStack AI-powered expert that provides respectful guidance on ritual etiquette, suggesting specific bundles or products based on the cultural occasion (e.g., house blessings or death anniversaries).
- **Remembrance Dashboard**: A personal registry where users create ancestral profiles, recording specific details like **Dialect Group (Hokkien, Teochew, etc.)**, relationships, and both birth and passing anniversaries.
- **Streamlined Checkout**: Unified flow for both physical product orders and membership-based proxy services.

### Free Membership (Basic)

- **Standard Calendar Access**: View upcoming public festivals and significant cultural dates.
- **Basic AI Guidance**: General heritage advice and product browsing.
- **Profile Management**: Basic ability to save preferences and ancestral names.

### Premium Membership ($12.90 / month)

- **Everything in Free**, plus:
- **Proxy Burning Services**: Professional fulfillment of rituals on your behalf, ideal for those unable to visit altars or columbariums personally.
- **Video Proof of Fulfillment**: Receive a recorded video confirmation showing the completion of your booked proxy ritual.
- **Advanced AI Personalization**: Deeper cultural context from the Advisor, including personalized bundle recommendations tailored to your family's specific dialect and traditions.
- **Automated Ritual Alerts**: Date reminders and actual-day notifications for personal anniversaries (birthdays/passing dates) of ancestors.
- **Enhanced History**: Access to a dashboard history of all past rituals and offering preferences.

## Owner Side

### Overall Features

- **Operational Dashboard**: Real-time monitoring of **Active Preorders**, **Weekly Revenue**, and **Service Queue** volume.
- **Proxy Queue Management**: A streamlined workflow to "Start Fulfillment", "Mark as Complete", and manage **Video Proof** uploads for members.
- **Deep Analytics & Insights**:
  - **Segment Retention**: Tracking loyalty and engagement across cultural groups: **Hokkien, Cantonese, Teochew, Hakka, and Hainanese**.
  - **Conversion Tracking**: Monitoring views vs. orders for specific kits like the _Hungry Ghost Bundle_.
- **AI-Driven Forecasting**: Predictive models for upcoming peak seasons (e.g., Qingming Cycle) showing **Demand Lift Percentages** and confidence levels to ensure stock readiness.
- **Inventory Control**: Live tracking of stock with a "Low Inventory Alert" system for critical items like incense and candles.
- **Operational Monitoring**: Tracking order dispatch status and in-flight resource allocation.
